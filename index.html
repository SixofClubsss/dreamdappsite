dReams is a open source platform application that houses multiple desktop dApps built on Dero. From games, to marketplaces, to blockchain services, to development tools. dReams has many avenues to explore.

Leveraging Dero's private L1, dReams gives users new ways to interact while preserving privacy. With features like encrypted messaging and local Gnomon DB, you are in control of your data.

The project originated in games development by integrating Dero games into a Fyne app. It was submitted in dArch Event 1 where it gained traction with other devs and began to integrate services. Written in Go, dReams source code aims to give developers easy access to tools for building Dero dApps. At this stage in development, dReams is available on Desktop only.

Timeline

Dero Stargate released to mainnet February 26, 2022. Stargate is built upon the first homomorphic encryption blockchain protocol. Developers and privacy advocates were quick putting it to use.

Originating in games creation, the project got its roots as an integration of decentralized Dero games into a desktop app using the Fyne Toolkit.

Leveraging ART-NFA-MS1, dReams becomes the first Dero app to integrate a NFA marketplace for its in game assets.

dReams was submitted in dArch Event 1 where it gained traction with other devs and began to integrate new Dero services and create new dApps, building off the projects framework.

After months of development, dReams and fellow developers took the grand prize for their collaborations. dReams version 0.9.5 was released, which included six dApp integrations and import packages for developers to build with.

Moving into the future, the project is working towards further dApp and asset collection integrations. As well as the continued expansion of the package imports to aid in facilitating further growth of the dReams and Dero ecosystems. Stay tuned for updates!
